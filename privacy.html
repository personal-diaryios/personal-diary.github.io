Privacy Policy

Please take a moment to familiarize yourself with our Privacy Policy. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) describes how we collect, use, and share information — including both personal and non-personal data — in connection with your use of our apps and services. By using our app, you agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

I. Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

We do not collect or store any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or any content stored on your device.

2. Non-Personal Information

We may collect non-personal information such as:

  • Device model and OS version
  • App usage statistics (e.g., which features are used most)

  • IP address (used for approximate location and compliance)

This information helps maintain and improve our services.

3. Session Data

Includes connectivity, error logs, access times, and general usage statistics. This does not include any personally identifying information or diary content.

4. Analytics Services

We may use analytics services such as:

  • Google Analytics / Firebase

These tools collect aggregated, anonymized data to help understand app usage.

5. Advertising & Cookies

Our free version may include ads via:

  • AdMob (Google)

These networks may use cookies or mobile ad identifiers to personalize ads. You can disable personalized ads in your device settings.

6. User Feedback

If you contact us, we may collect your email address and message contents — used solely to respond or provide support.

II. How We Use Information

We primarily use non-personal data to:

  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Understand user behavior to enhance features
  • Generate anonymous internal statistics

III. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent any data. We may share anonymized data with:

  • Analytics service providers
  • Advertising networks (for ad-supported apps)
  • Platform providers (Apple, Google, etc.)

Please review their privacy policies:

  • Google / Firebase / AdMob
  • Facebook Analytics & Audience Network

IV. Security

We use reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect data. However, no system is 100% secure. Use the app at your own discretion.

V. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided information, please contact us to remove it.

VI. Your Rights

Users in the EU or California may have rights under GDPR or CCPA to access or delete data. Since we do not collect personal data, most of these rights do not apply. Still, you may contact us with any privacy-related requests.

VII. Third-Party Services

Our app may use or link to third-party services that operate under their own privacy policies. Please review those policies before using those services.
