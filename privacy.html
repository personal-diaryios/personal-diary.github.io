Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) describes how we collect, use, and share information — including both personal and non-personal data — in connection with your use of our app Personal Diary – Mood Journal. By using the app, you agree to this Policy.

I. Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

We do not collect or store personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or diary contents.

2. Non-Personal Information

We may collect limited non-personal data to help improve the app, such as:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App usage statistics (for example, which features are used most)
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • IP address (used only for approximate region and compliance)

3. Session Data

Includes general usage details such as connection status, access times, and feature interactions. This does not include any diary or personal content.

4. Analytics Services

We may use third-party analytics tools like Google Analytics or Firebase to understand general usage patterns. This data is aggregated and anonymous.

5. Advertising

The free version of the app may include ads from networks such as Google AdMob or Facebook Audience Network. These partners may use device identifiers or cookies for ad personalization. You can turn off personalized ads in your device settings.

6. User Feedback

If you contact us for support, your message and email address will be used only to respond to your inquiry.

II. How We Use Information

  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Understand how users interact with the app
  • Fix crashes or bugs
  • Enhance user experience and plan new features

III. Local Storage

Your diary entries, photos, moods, and tags are stored locally on your device. This data is never uploaded to any external server unless you explicitly choose to back up using iCloud.

IV. iCloud Backup (Optional)

If you enable iCloud Backup, your diary data is stored securely within your personal iCloud account associated with your Apple ID. We (the app developers) do not have access to this data. All iCloud operations are managed by Apple’s CloudKit and are subject to Apple’s Privacy Policy.

V. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information. We may share anonymized or aggregated data with:

  • Analytics providers
  • Advertising networks (for free versions)
  • Platform providers such as Apple or Google

VI. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect your information. However, no digital system is completely secure. Please use the app responsibly and avoid storing highly sensitive information.

VII. Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe that a child has provided information, please contact us and we will delete it.

VIII. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Users in certain regions such as the European Union or California may have data rights (access, deletion, etc.). Since we do not collect personal data, most of these rights do not apply. However, you can still contact us for any privacy-related requests.

IX. Third-Party Services

The app may link to or use third-party services (e.g., Apple, Google, Facebook). These operate under their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their terms before use.

X. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be available in the app or on our official website. Continued use of the app means you accept any changes.
